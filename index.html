Fundamentals of Applied Cognitive Neuroscience

Bangladesh’s first open, online, Bengali-first course introducing cognitive neuroscience through applied research, experiments, and brain data.

12 Sessions Project-Based Beginner Friendly Bengali-First

About the Program

Fundamentals of Applied Cognitive Neuroscience is a structured online program designed to introduce learners to how cognition is studied scientifically. The course integrates behavioral experimentation, foundational EEG concepts, and scientific reasoning to help learners understand the brain–mind–behavior relationship.

This program is intentionally designed for learners who do not have access to neuroscience laboratories, advanced coursework, or formal neuroscience degrees. Instruction is delivered primarily in Bengali to ensure accessibility and clarity.

Why This Course Is Important

Access Gap

Cognitive neuroscience education in Bangladesh is limited to selected institutions. This course opens access to learners nationwide.

Applied Focus

Learners move beyond theory to understand how experiments and brain data are actually used in research.

Research Orientation

The program emphasizes scientific thinking, interpretation, and ethical research practice.

This is a foundational-level applied course, not a substitute for graduate or clinical neuroscience training.

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand core concepts of cognitive neuroscience
  • Explain how cognition is studied experimentally
  • Design basic cognitive tasks
  • Interpret EEG and ERP findings at a foundational level
  • Read and understand neuroscience research figures
  • Integrate behavioral and neural evidence responsibly

Program Structure

  • Total sessions: 12
  • Schedule: 2 sessions per week
  • Duration: 6–7 weeks
  • Mode: Online (Live)

Group Project

Learners will work in small groups on a guided mini-project involving behavioral task design or ERP analysis using open datasets. The project emphasizes interpretation, not advanced technical execution.

Frequently Asked Questions

No. This course is designed for beginners.

No. This is a fundamentals-level applied program.

Will I learn advanced EEG preprocessing?

No. The focus is on understanding and interpretation.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes. An e-certificate will be issued by BIIHR upon completion.

Is the course fully in Bengali?

Yes, with necessary technical terms in English.
